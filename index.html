main halls

halls

our school has many halls

The Gamaliel Hall is named after our longest serving Principal, the Late Mr. Lazarus Gamaliel. It now serves as a multimedia centre with professional lighting and sound. The daily morning assemblies, the Inter-House Competitions, seminars and workshops for staff and parents and get-togethers are held in the Gamaliel Hall in air-conditioned comfort. The Mackay Hall, named after our first Principal Mr. Adam Mackay, was formerly the chapel. The Mackay Hall is fully air conditioned and is now equipped with multimedia facilities for the students. The hall is also utilized for meetings, presentations and curricular and co-curricular activities. The Andrew’s Hall, built to commemorate the 150th year of the school, was inaugurated by then Governor of Mumbai, Dr. P.C. Alexander. The morning assemblies, PT classes and after school co-curricular activities are held in this open-aired hall.
